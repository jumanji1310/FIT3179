Average broadband and mobile speed from 2020 to 2021

Broadband price by country in 2022

The scatterplot displays the average broadband and mobile download speed in Megabits per second with broadband speed by Ookla in 2021 and mobile speed by Opensignal in 2020. The highest mobile and broadband speed for each continent is shown when filtered. Australia has the highest average mobile speed at 100Mbits/s and ranked 41st in the world for broadband speed with 77.88Mbits/s. The highest broadband speed belongs to Singapore with an average speed of 245.5Mbits/s.

The highest and lowest average broadband price in AUD for unlimited data at 60Mbytes/s or more for each country in 2022 is shown above. United Arab Emirates has the most expensive broadband at $153.88 AUD while Ukraine has the cheapest at $6.93 AUD. Australia is ranked 16th in the world at $76.52 AUD and is highlighted in red.

From the chart above, the number of subscribers for different connection types from 2013 to 2018 bi-annually in Australia can be compared. The chart can be filtered to show a specific broadband type and allow trends to be seen. Digital subscriber line or DSL subscribers in Australia has slowly declined while fibre users has increased exponentially throughout the years. The number of Cable and Mobile connection users have stayed relatively constant throughout the years.
